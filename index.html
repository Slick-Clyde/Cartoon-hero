Donald Duck

About Donald

Donald is one of the main classic Disney characters. He trys to be a good uncle to his nephews and gentleman to his love Daisy which mostly does not work out.

Donald buying some flowers for his crush Daisy

Characteristics

  1. Always wears blue hat
  2. Good heart but very gawky
  3. Sometimes hot temper
  4. Usually broke
